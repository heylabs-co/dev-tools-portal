# Developer Tools Affiliate & Referral Programs

> Last updated: April 2026
> Sorted by ease of joining (easiest first)

---

## TIER 1: EASY TO JOIN (Self-service signup, no approval barriers)

### 1. DigitalOcean
- **Program URL:** https://www.digitalocean.com/affiliates
- **Commission type:** Recurring % of spend
- **Commission amount:** 10% recurring for 12 months on referred customer's monthly spend
- **Cookie duration:** 30 days
- **Requirements:** None -- anyone can join
- **How to sign up:** Self-service via CJ Affiliate (Commission Junction)
- **Network:** CJ Affiliate
- **Min payout:** $50
- **Ease of joining:** EASY -- open to all, no approval needed
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** $200-500/mo (assuming 0.5% conversion, avg $50/mo spend)

### 2. Netlify
- **Program URL:** https://www.netlify.com/partners/ (via PartnerStack)
- **Commission type:** Recurring % revenue share
- **Commission amount:** 20% revenue share for up to 12 months (Ecosystem tier); 20% for up to 24 months (Certified tier)
- **Cookie duration:** Standard PartnerStack tracking
- **Requirements:** None for Ecosystem tier (starting level). Certified tier requires 3+ Pro or 1+ Enterprise referrals/year
- **How to sign up:** Self-service via PartnerStack application
- **Network:** PartnerStack
- **Ease of joining:** EASY -- anyone can apply as Ecosystem Partner
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** $150-400/mo (Netlify Pro is $19/mo per member)

### 3. Vercel (v0 Affiliate)
- **Program URL:** https://vercel.com/legal/affiliate-marketing-terms
- **Commission type:** Hybrid (flat fee per lead + recurring %)
- **Commission amount:** $5 per lead + 30% recurring for first 6 months on Premium/Team subscriptions
- **Cookie duration:** Not publicly disclosed (see Program Guidelines)
- **Requirements:** Must have active Vercel account in good standing; acceptance at Vercel's discretion
- **How to sign up:** Application-based, but relatively open
- **Network:** Direct / Vercel proprietary
- **Ease of joining:** EASY-MEDIUM -- application required but broadly accessible
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** $300-600/mo (leads + recurring on v0 Premium at $20/mo)

### 4. Algolia
- **Program URL:** https://partners.algolia.com/become-a-partner
- **Commission type:** % of first year revenue
- **Commission amount:** Up to 15% on referred customer's first year with Algolia
- **Cookie duration:** Not publicly disclosed
- **Requirements:** Partner application
- **How to sign up:** Self-service application via partner portal
- **Network:** Direct
- **Ease of joining:** EASY-MEDIUM -- open application
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** $100-300/mo (Algolia plans start at $0, paid plans from ~$100/mo)

### 5. Neon (Postgres)
- **Program URL:** https://neon.com/programs/open-source (OSS program); https://neon.com/blog/partner-program (partner)
- **Commission type:** Flat fee per referral (OSS program)
- **Commission amount:** $10 per referred user who spends $10+ (no cap on earnings)
- **Cookie duration:** Not disclosed
- **Requirements:** For OSS program: have an open-source project. Partner program: apply via form
- **How to sign up:** Self-service for OSS; application for partner
- **Network:** Direct (payouts via GitHub Sponsors for OSS)
- **Ease of joining:** EASY for OSS maintainers; MEDIUM for others
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** $50-150/mo (low average conversion given free tier)

## TIER 2: MODERATE (Application with lightweight review)

### 6. Vanta
- **Program URL:** https://help.vanta.com/en/articles/11345414-vanta-customer-referral-program
- **Commission type:** Flat fee per referral
- **Commission amount:** $500 Visa Gift Card per successful referral (referred customer gets $1,000 off first year)
- **Cookie duration:** Not applicable (manual referral tracking)
- **Requirements:** Must be an existing Vanta customer
- **How to sign up:** Customer referral -- must already use Vanta
- **Network:** Direct
- **Ease of joining:** MEDIUM -- must be a Vanta customer first
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** Hard to estimate -- enterprise product, low volume but $500/referral is significant

### 7. Clerk
- **Program URL:** https://clerk.com/creators
- **Commission type:** Sponsorship / partnership (not traditional affiliate)
- **Commission amount:** Not publicly disclosed; includes free Clerk Pro access, swag, dedicated support
- **Cookie duration:** N/A
- **Requirements:** Must be a content creator (YouTube, newsletter, podcast, etc.)
- **How to sign up:** Application form on creators page
- **Network:** Direct partnership
- **Ease of joining:** MEDIUM -- need to be an active content creator; reviewed case by case
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** Varies (sponsorship model rather than per-click affiliate)

### 8. RevenueCat
- **Program URL:** https://www.revenuecat.com/partners/
- **Commission type:** Mixed (% of first-year subscription + tiered bonuses for self-serve)
- **Commission amount:** % of first-year subscription value (exact % in Partner Hub after approval)
- **Cookie duration:** Not publicly disclosed
- **Requirements:** Partner application reviewed by team
- **How to sign up:** Application via PartnerStack
- **Network:** PartnerStack
- **Ease of joining:** MEDIUM -- requires approval
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** $100-300/mo (RevenueCat pricing scales with app revenue)

### 9. Okta / Auth0
- **Program URL:** https://www.okta.com/customer-referral-program/ (Okta); https://auth0.com/partners (Auth0)
- **Commission type:** Flat fee per enterprise deal (Okta); revenue share (Auth0)
- **Commission amount:** $100-500 per enterprise referral (Okta); margins on resell/referral (Auth0 -- exact % not public)
- **Cookie duration:** Not disclosed
- **Requirements:** Existing customer (Okta referral); partner application (Auth0)
- **How to sign up:** Customer referral form (Okta); partner application (Auth0)
- **Network:** Direct
- **Ease of joining:** MEDIUM -- Okta requires being a customer; Auth0 requires partner approval
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** Low volume, high value -- enterprise sales cycle

## TIER 3: HARDER (Enterprise/B2B partner programs, significant requirements)

### 10. Stripe
- **Program URL:** https://stripe.com/partners/become-a-partner
- **Commission type:** Revenue share on referred processing volume
- **Commission amount:** Not publicly disclosed -- paid when referred businesses actively process payments
- **Cookie duration:** Not publicly disclosed
- **Requirements:** Must be a developer, agency, SaaS company, or platform actively integrating Stripe. NOT for casual bloggers or banner ads
- **How to sign up:** Application to Stripe Partner Ecosystem
- **Network:** Stripe Connect (direct)
- **Ease of joining:** HARD -- requires demonstrating active integration work; not a traditional affiliate program
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** Potentially high if referring businesses with significant payment volume

### 11. Twilio / SendGrid
- **Program URL:** https://www.twilio.com/en-us/partners/become-a-partner
- **Commission type:** % of minimum spend
- **Commission amount:** 10% of Minimum Spend Amount (min $25K/yr deal), max cap $500K
- **Cookie duration:** N/A (deal registration model)
- **Requirements:** Must be in top partner tiers; order term min 12 months
- **How to sign up:** Partner application
- **Network:** Direct
- **Ease of joining:** HARD -- enterprise-focused, requires partner tier status
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** N/A for content sites -- designed for B2B deal referrals

### 12. Datadog
- **Program URL:** https://www.datadoghq.com/partner/network/
- **Commission type:** Referral fee (exact structure not public)
- **Commission amount:** Not publicly disclosed -- "competitive incentives"
- **Cookie duration:** N/A (deal registration)
- **Requirements:** Must apply as MSP, SI, Reseller, Referral Partner, or Technology Partner
- **How to sign up:** Partner Network application
- **Network:** Direct
- **Ease of joining:** HARD -- enterprise partner program with approval process
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** N/A for content sites

### 13. Snyk
- **Program URL:** https://partners.snyk.io/
- **Commission type:** Channel partner referral (exact structure not public)
- **Commission amount:** Not publicly disclosed
- **Cookie duration:** N/A
- **Requirements:** Channel partner application; Ambassador program available for advocates
- **How to sign up:** Partner portal application
- **Network:** Direct
- **Ease of joining:** HARD -- enterprise partner focus
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** N/A for content sites

### 14. Grafana Labs
- **Program URL:** https://grafana.com/partnerships/
- **Commission type:** Referral fee + reseller margins
- **Commission amount:** Not publicly disclosed -- "pricing incentives" and "upsell opportunities"
- **Cookie duration:** N/A
- **Requirements:** Channel/reseller partner application
- **How to sign up:** Partner portal at partners.grafana.com
- **Network:** Direct
- **Ease of joining:** HARD -- B2B channel partner focus
- **Est. revenue at 10K visitors/mo:** N/A for content sites

## TIER 4: NO PUBLIC AFFILIATE PROGRAM

### 15. Cloudflare
- **Status:** NO public affiliate program
- **What exists:** PowerUP Partner Program (channel partners), Agency Program (agencies/hosts)
- **URL:** https://www.cloudflare.com/partners/
- **Notes:** B2B channel partner program only. No self-serve affiliate signups. Must be a reseller, agency, or MSP

### 16. Supabase
- **Status:** NO traditional affiliate program
- **What exists:** Technology partner program (integrations), SupaSquad (community advocates -- non-monetary)
- **URL:** https://supabase.com/partners
- **Notes:** SupaSquad is an advocacy/community program, not commission-based. Tech partnerships focus on product integrations

### 17. MongoDB
- **Status:** NO public affiliate program
- **What exists:** Enterprise partner program (SIs, resellers, technology partners)
- **URL:** https://www.mongodb.com/company/partners
- **Notes:** Compensation via deal registration only. Designed for enterprise sales partners, not content creators

### 18. Sentry
- **Status:** NO public affiliate program (for Sentry.io)
- **What exists:** Partnership platform for hosting providers and developer platforms. Listed on Awin network but details unclear
- **URL:** https://docs.sentry.io/product/partnership-platform/
- **Notes:** Partnership is for platforms integrating Sentry, not for content affiliates

### 19. PostHog
- **Status:** NO affiliate program
- **What exists:** Startup referral program ($10K credits split between referrer and referee)
- **URL:** https://posthog.com/handbook/brand/partners
- **Notes:** Explicitly states they do not operate an affiliate program. Startup credit program only

### 20. Paddle
- **Status:** NO in-house affiliate program (discontinued)
- **What exists:** Third-party affiliate management integrations (Rewardful, Partnero, Tolt)
- **URL:** https://www.paddle.com/ (no direct affiliate page)
- **Notes:** Paddle removed their built-in affiliate feature. They are a payment platform, not an affiliate program provider

### 21. PlanetScale
- **Status:** NO public affiliate program found
- **URL:** https://planetscale.com/
- **Notes:** No evidence of an affiliate or referral program in any public documentation

### 22. LaunchDarkly
- **Status:** NO public affiliate program
- **What exists:** Technology partner program
- **URL:** https://launchdarkly.com/partners
- **Notes:** Partner program focused on technology integrations, not content affiliate commissions

### 23. BrowserStack
- **Status:** NO cash affiliate program
- **What exists:** Referral program that rewards free minutes (not cash). Partner program (contact for details)
- **URL:** https://www.browserstack.com/partners
- **Notes:** Referral gives 100 free App-Live minutes per referral. Cash commissions require direct partner negotiation

### 24. CircleCI
- **Status:** NO public affiliate program
- **What exists:** Partner program for VARs, resellers, referral partners
- **URL:** https://circleci.com/partners/
- **Notes:** No self-serve affiliate signup. Partner program requires direct engagement

### 25. New Relic
- **Status:** NO active public affiliate program (had one historically, launched 2008)
- **What exists:** Partner Program for observability
- **URL:** https://newrelic.com/solutions/partners
- **Notes:** Historical program paid first month of subscription. Current program is enterprise partner focused

## RECOMMENDED ACTION PLAN

### Immediate (sign up today):
1. **DigitalOcean** -- Best overall for developer content. Self-serve CJ signup, 10% recurring, no barriers
2. **Netlify** -- 20% rev share via PartnerStack, easy application
3. **Vercel** -- Hybrid model with good upside if you have a developer audience

### Short-term (apply this week):
4. **Algolia** -- 15% first-year commission, solid for search-related content
5. **Neon** -- Easy for OSS maintainers, $10/referral with no cap

### Evaluate further:
6. **RevenueCat** -- Good if your audience builds mobile apps
7. **Clerk** -- Creator partnership if you produce video/newsletter content
8. **Vanta** -- High per-referral payout if you reach compliance/security buyers

### Skip for now (not worth the effort for content sites):
- Enterprise-only programs: Stripe, Twilio, Datadog, Snyk, Grafana
- No program: Cloudflare, Supabase, MongoDB, Sentry, PostHog, PlanetScale, LaunchDarkly, BrowserStack, CircleCI, New Relic
